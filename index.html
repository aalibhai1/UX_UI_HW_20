Foodie Friends App

Project Overview

Foodies need an easier way to try new foods and resturants when their peers can't join them in the experience.

Solution: Connect like-minded individuals based on food and resturant preferences

My Role: UX/UI Designer

Time Frame: October 2020

Class:UX/UI, Georgia Institute of Technology

Team: 4 person group project

Tools: Figma, Adobe XD, InVision, Miro, Google Suite, Trello

Research

16%

Conduct user interviews via zoom and surveys via social media. Within the user-interviews, answer the established research questions.Participants should be indivudals 18-37 of age. Indivduals who are interested in making connections and trying new food.

Definition & Synthesis

33%
It was understanding the problem statement that was created via the user-persona. Through I like/I wish/Whatif, we decided to use dot voting to agree as a group which features we wanted to include in our app. We began focusing on the features that were the highest of priority and lowest complexity.

Ideation

50%

Our storyboard told a story, how our user-persona (Michelle) is dying to try a new restaurant; however, her husband has been issued. She had a problem and a solution brought by her friends in our app (Foodie Friends).
